The NASA-Cosmoboard Space Biology Dashboard is part of the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge. It aims to provide scientists, mission planners, and managers with a dynamic interface to explore and summarize decades of NASA bioscience publications. This project presents a user-friendly layout for accessing all related research with Space Biology topics and recommended papers to help aid in research based on keywords in your selected paper and the data sets that were used to create the paper.
